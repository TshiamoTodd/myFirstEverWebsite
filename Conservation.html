Importance of conservation

conservation means we are able to switch on our lights 10/15 years from now.
Electricity is the key to men's survival if we overly exert our resorces
to create electricity we are harming the Earth as well as redusing the availability
of electricity for ourselves for years to come.

Conserving electricity starts with YOU telling yourself that the future
is more important than today. If you have the sel-motivation to conserve electricity and
reduce load shedding, you will lead a movement to better South Africa because people only
only do good when they see you do good.

  • Use your electricity wisely
  • Always encourage others to save electricity
  • Never leave your house without switching off non-operational applaiences
  • Keep your pool pump off when the pool is not at use
  • Switch off geyser at night
  • Convert your house to run on solar energy
  • Always have a backup electrical supply plan
  • Pray that Eskom will never run out of coal to burn
